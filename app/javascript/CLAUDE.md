Bundled with esbuild. Stimulus controllers for all JS behavior.

## Key Controllers

- `controllers/app_wake_controller.js` — keeps app connection alive
- `controllers/bot/*_controller.js` — bot-specific interactions
- `controllers/form/*_controller.js` — form handling
- `controllers/hotwire_animations_controller.js` — Turbo Frame animations
- `tauri.js` — IPC bridge for Tauri desktop app
